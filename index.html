I am an AI Resident at Meta FAIR (previously Facebook AI Research), where I am exploring ideas to improve systematic generalization of Reinforcement Learning agents. Previously, I spent a wonderful year at CMU (albeit remotely) writing my undergraduate thesis under Katerina Fragkiadaki on topics in 3D Computer Vision and Language Grounding. I recently completed my undergraduate studies at BITS Pilani, majoring in Computer Science.
